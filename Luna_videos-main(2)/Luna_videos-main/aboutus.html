About Sun NXT

Sun NXT is an online video streaming platform owned and operated by Sun TV Network Limited which offers over 50,000 hours of live TV content, movies, originals, kid’s Content, music across 6 Indian languages viz: Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Bengali & Marathi.
High level audio/video streaming technology and a high attention to quality of user experience across devices and platforms makes Sun NXT the most complete entertainment destination for Over the Top (OTT) audio/video consumers.
